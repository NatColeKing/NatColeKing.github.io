VERSE is a recreation of an Elizabethan theater in virtual reality, based primarily on Shakespeare's Globe in London. Created in collaboration with Santa Clara University's English Department, this experience serves to teach users what an Elizabethan theater might have looked like and what individual components made up the theater.
This project was created over a year with 3 other students. The wizarding world of TAVRN begins in our tavern, which serves as the game's main menu. From the tavern, users can race through our medieval town on brooms, or fight other wizards in our spell duel. TAVRN is now available to try on the Oculus store.
Trivial Pursuit is the story of an awkward robot surviving a mugging by a clan of mean-spirited robots. This project was made with 2 other students over the course of two months.
