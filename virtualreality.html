VERSE, the Virtual Eglish Renaissance Stage Experience, is an accurate architectural recreation of an Elizabethan theater in virtual reality. Based primarily on Shakespeare's Globe in London, this VR experience was created in collaboration with Santa Clara University's English Department to teach students what Elizabethan theaters looked like what individual components made up the theater. The model was made and UVed in Autodesk Maya, textured with Substance Designer and Painter, and then brought into Unity. Click here to read more.
Gift of Life is a non-profit that matches bone marrow and stem cell donors to patients with blood-borne illnesses like leukemia. aVResis is designed for potential donors to better understand the stem cell donation process so they'll be more likely to join the registry. Users go through a full donation and a roller- coaster-like ride through the apheresis machine, including the centrifuge and blood-warmer. I modeled and textured the apheresis machine, donation chair, and all other furniture in Autodesk Maya, put the project together in Unity, and used Cinemachine to create a track through the apheresis machine. Offworld is a virtual reality cyberpunk space-racer created by a group of eight students over five months. Players can choose to fly one of our five ships and race against the other four.
This project was created over a year with three other students. The wizarding world of TAVRN begins in our tavern, which serves as the game's main menu. From the tavern, users can race through our medieval town on brooms, or fight other wizards in our spell duel. TAVRN is now available to try on the Oculus store.
Trivial Pursuit is the story of an awkward robot surviving a mugging by a clan of mean-spirited robots. This project was made with two other students over the course of two months.
Taiko uses 360 videos to digitally tell the story of a Japanese drumming group based in San Jose, CA. The videos were brought together in unity and made viewable in virtual reality. This project was completed over the course of two months with the help of two other students.
